Time Travel Chronicles

In this original game, players use time travel and supernatural powers to help cities in different time periods by utilizing natural resources and elemental abilities to combat natural disasters, and directing resources towards sustainable technologies like wind turbines, solar panels, and hydropower. Inspired by Minecraft, Quantum Leap, and the Avatar: the Last Airbender, this game was initially created to advocate for awareness for environmental issues and solutions.

Facial Recognition Model Comparsions

During the Summer Research and Innovation Program at NCSSM, I learned about the discrepancies and bias in machine learning and AI. Eventually, I created facial recognition models using datasets of 16,130 animal species images to evaluate the accuracy of Eigenfaces, FisherFaces, and Local Binary Pattern Histograms, and presented the findings.
